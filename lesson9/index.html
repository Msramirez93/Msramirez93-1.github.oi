Tropical storm warning for northern Gulf Coast, with flooding likely through Saturday

By Ian Livingston ,June 18, 2021 at 2:07 p.m. GMT-3

A developing tropical storm is headed toward the northern Gulf Coast, where it is expected to unload up to a foot of flooding rain. Landfall of the storm system is expected late Friday night into Saturday morning along the Louisiana shoreline. A tropical storm warning is in effect for nearly 7 million people, including the populations of New Orleans; Mobile, Ala.; and Biloxi, Miss.
